About CultivatED

CultivatED is an EDTech advanced personalized learning website, dedicated to a Phase 1 focus on Standardized Test Prep, with an ethos to disseminate curated and quality education to underrepresented demographics in education, diverging from the normalized Eurocentric standard of learning which marginalizes so many students internationally. CultivatED uses a diagnostic test to gather both qualitative and quantitative data about students. By leveraging machine learning, it places students into tailored learning tropes and assigns customized test prep modules designed to meet their specific needs.

Who We Serve

  • Students from low-access or resource-constrained communities
  • International students facing educational barriers
  • Underrepresented minorities in education

How It Works

  1. Take our diagnostic assessment
  2. Get matched with a personalized learning path
  3. Access customized SAT prep modules
  4. Track your progress and improve your scores
