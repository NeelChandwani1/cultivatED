About cultivatED

cultivatED is on a mission to democratize SAT preparation by providing free, high-quality resources to students in underdeveloped countries. We believe that access to education should not be limited by geographical or financial constraints.

Our comprehensive program includes interactive lessons, practice tests, and personalized tutoring to help students achieve their full potential on the SAT and beyond.

Join our mission to make SAT prep accessible to students worldwide. Interested in volunteering or partnering with us? Get in touch to learn more about opportunities to contribute to our cause.
